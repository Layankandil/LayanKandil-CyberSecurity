UJ PathFinder

UJ PathFinder is an exclusive application for the University of Jeddah community that revolutionizes the academic journey by providing a centralized platform for students, alumni, faculty, and companies. It allows students to manage profiles showcasing their experiences, request graduation documents and recommendations, and connect with others while serving as a bridge to job opportunities. Companies can view student profiles and engage in training programs, ensuring a seamless integration of education and industry. Additionally, the app displays the employment rate of graduates, offering insights into alumni success, and mandates that companies allocate at least 2% of their workforce for annual student training opportunities, fostering valuable internship experiences and mutual growth within the UJ PathFinder platform
